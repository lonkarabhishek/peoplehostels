The People Hostels: Where Luxury Meets Tradition

Nestled in the heart of historical wonders, The People Hostels is not just a stay but an experience that blends the grace of the Mughal era with contemporary comforts. Just a 15-minute stroll from the City Palace, and a short drive from the enchanting Lake Pichola and the blooming Saheliyon-ki-Bari garden, our hotel is a sanctuary in the bustling city. Each room, adorned with hand-painted furniture and traditional textiles, echoes the region's rich heritage, complemented by modern amenities like free Wi-Fi and air-conditioning. Savor a culinary journey on our rooftop terrace, offering an array of regional and international flavors amidst panoramic views. The serene walled garden and the fountain courtyard provide perfect retreats within the city. Indulge in personalized experiences with our massage services and intriguing palm reading sessions, ensuring every moment at The People Hostels is memorable.
